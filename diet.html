BULKING

SUMMARY

Bulking = best way to build muscle faster (better than “maingaining”)

  • 🍽️ Eat in a small calorie surplus about 5–10% above maintenance (not dirty bulking)
  • Control weight gain aim for 1–2% body weight per month to avoid excess fat
  • High protein and balanced macros = Protein is key, but carbs help performance
  • Train hard and progressively overload, bulking without proper training = just getting fat
  • Cardio is still useful helps health and doesn’t kill gains if moderate


Cutting

SUMMARY

  • 💡 You don’t need tons of sets to grow even low volume (1–2 hard sets) can build muscle if intensity is high Intensity matters more than volume, training close to failure = biggest growth driver
  • Both low and high volume can work, the “best” depends on:
    1. Your recovery
    2. Experience level
    3. Time available
    4. Efficiency is key
  • Fewer, harder sets = similar results with less time

STRICT RULES

Food Essentials

  • Small calorie surplus: don’t dirty bulk
  • Protein daily: 1.6–2.2g per kg bodyweight
  • Carbs = energy, don’t cut them, they fuel workouts
  • Eat consistently: not just “when you feel like it”
  • Hydrate: water affects strength + pumps
  • Track progress: scale and mirror (adjust if gaining too fast)

Gym Essentials

  • Progressive overload: increase the weight and reps over time
  • Train close to failure, the last reps should be HARD
  • Good form > ego lifting
  • Full range of motion: stretch = more growth
  • Hit muscles 2x per week
  • Rest is important: muscles grow outside the gym
  • Be consistent: results come from weeks, not days
